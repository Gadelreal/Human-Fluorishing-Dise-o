Welcome to Human Flourishing

Embark on a transformative journey to understand the science and practice of well-being. This course will guide you through evidence-based strategies for living with greater awareness, purpose, and resilience.

Course Overview

About This Course

Human Flourishing is designed to help you understand and apply the science of well-being in your daily life. Through five comprehensive lessons, you'll explore what it means to be human, develop greater self-awareness, discover your purpose, build resilience, and create sustainable practices for lasting positive change.

What You'll Learn

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

Understand Human Nature

Appreciate the complexity of being human, including our need for connection and the role of biases in our daily lives.

Cultivate Awareness

Develop emotional granularity, understand metacognition, and harness the power of positive emotions for personal growth.

Find Your Purpose

Identify your core values, explore your ikigai, and understand the difference between intrinsic and extrinsic motivation.

Build Resilience

Learn an evolved definition of resilience, identify your character strengths, and develop strategies for overcoming challenges.

Take Ownership

Create sustainable practices, establish accountability systems, and take ownership of your flourishing journey.

Apply Practical Tools

Gain evidence-based strategies and practical exercises you can implement immediately in your personal and professional life.
