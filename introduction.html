Course Description and Learning Objectives

Course Description

Digital Well-being is a course designed to develop awareness and practices that help us leverage technology to enhance our lives, while also fostering human flourishing, connection, innovation, and productivity.

Technological advances have transformed the way we live, work, and communicate, becoming omnipresent in our daily lives. These shifts have made movement, connection, and communication more accessible. However, data shows that our personal habits—both at work and in life—can contribute to increased isolation, reduced empathy, and diminished self-awareness.

This course, developed by the Center for Health and Well-Being at IE University, takes a proactive approach to cultivating awareness and applying scientific insights to develop healthy practices that support brain function, relationships, and productivity through balanced technology use. We will explore learning at an individual level while also reflecting on the digital culture we are collectively shaping.

At the Center for Health and Well-Being, we embrace a holistic approach to flourishing and well-being. As technology becomes ever more integral to life, we aim to foster greater appreciation, learning, perspective, and balance in our digital well- being.

Course Learning Objectives

Digital Well-being Appreciation

Develop an appreciation for digital well-being and understand its importance

Technology's Impact

Recognize the ways technology influences our physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being

Behavioral Understanding

Understand how behaviors and habits related to technology affect cognitive functioning, productivity, and relationships

Holistic Approach

Cultivate a holistic and proactive approach to establish practices that support cognition, productivity, and balance in technology use

Digital Thriving

Strengthen the ability to thrive both personally and professionally in a digital environment

Personal Action Plan

Create a personalized digital well-being action plan

Meet the Minds Behind the Course

This course was created with care and expertise by professors from the IE University community, in collaboration with the IE Center for Health and Well-Being. Each contributor brings a deep passion for helping individuals lead more balanced, intentional, and fulfilling lives—both online and offline.

Their insights are rooted in science, shaped by experience, and guided by a shared commitment to personal and professional well-being.

We hope you enjoy the content and make the most of this journey. May it inspire meaningful reflection, spark new habits, and support your continued growth in digital well-being.
