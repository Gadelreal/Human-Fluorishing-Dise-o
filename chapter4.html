Introduction

This chapter covers the basics of responsive design. We'll explore how to create websites that adapt to different screen sizes and devices.

Media Queries

Media queries are a key component of responsive design. They allow you to apply different styles based on the characteristics of the device, such as screen width, height, and orientation.
